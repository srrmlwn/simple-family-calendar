Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

famcal.ai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our calendar application, including our website at famcal.ai and our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)
  • First and last name
  • Optional profile information

Calendar Data

  • Event details (title, description, date/time, location)
  • Event participants
  • Event status and preferences
  • Calendar settings and preferences

Contact Information

  • Email addresses of family members and contacts
  • Names of family members and contacts
  • Default recipient lists

Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Time zone
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Click patterns

Cookies and Similar Technologies

  • Session cookies for authentication
  • Persistent cookies for preferences
  • Local storage for offline functionality

Information from Third Parties

Google Sign-In

When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Profile picture (if provided by Google)
  • Google account ID

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Creating and managing your calendar
  • Sending event invitations and updates
  • Processing event notifications
  • Managing user preferences

Authentication and Security

  • Verifying your identity
  • Protecting against unauthorized access
  • Maintaining account security
  • Detecting and preventing fraud

Communication

  • Sending event notifications
  • Delivering calendar invitations
  • Providing service updates
  • Responding to support requests

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Optimizing application performance
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Developing new features

Google OAuth 2.0 Specific Information

Data Collection

  • We collect basic profile information provided by Google
  • We do not access or store your Google account password
  • We only request necessary permissions for calendar functionality

Data Usage

  • We use Google account information for authentication
  • We do not share your Google account information with third parties
  • We comply with Google's OAuth 2.0 requirements

User Control

  • You can revoke our access to your Google account at any time
  • You can manage your Google account permissions
  • You can delete your account and associated data
