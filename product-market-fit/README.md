## Core Thesis

Family calendar is a large, underserved category:
- Google Calendar is generic — no concept of family members as first-class entities
- Cozi proved the demand but hasn't innovated in years
- AI + multi-channel input (WhatsApp, SMS) applied to family scheduling is wide open

**The bet:** natural language becomes the primary interface, not a feature. Families interact with famcal.ai the same way they communicate with each other — in plain language, via messaging.

---

## The One-Line Pitch

> famcal.ai is the family calendar that understands you — add events by talking to it, get the whole family's week at a glance, and never miss a school pickup again.

---

## Key Differentiators (Target State)

1. **NLP-first** — every interaction can be done in natural language
2. **Family members as first-class entities** — events belong to people, not just calendars
3. **Multi-channel input** — WhatsApp, SMS, email, voice, photo — meet families where they already are
4. **Family-wide views** — see who has what, when conflicts exist, who needs a ride
5. **Smart notifications** — daily digest, weekly briefing, conflict alerts
