# App Name Research — famcal.ai

> Goal: Find a name that signals AI, simplifies life for families, and evokes happiness, smiles, and ease.

---

## Naming Criteria

| Criteria | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Signals AI | .ai TLD or AI-adjacent feel; product is AI-native |
| Family-coded | Parents should instantly feel it's for them |
| Happiness / warmth | Smiles, brightness, joy — the app reduces family stress |
| Short & memorable | ≤ 8 chars ideal; easy to say aloud |
| Clean availability | No active competing products; domain registrable |

---

## Session 1 — Initial Ideas

Starting point: **mivida.ai** ("my life" in Spanish — warmth, personal, family)
→ **Taken.** Multiple active products (Peruvian health clinic, fitness club, property management app).

Other early candidates explored: `zevive`, `joiful`, `lumio`, `happio`, `heyday`
→ Most were taken (see full research below).

---

## Recommendation

**smilecal.ai** is the strongest candidate right now:
- Communicates the product (calendar) directly
- Signals positive emotion (smile) — the promise of family happiness
- .ai TLD signals AI-native
- No competing product found
- Easy to say, spell, and remember

**famglow.ai** is the best runner-up if you want something less literal and more brand-poetic.
