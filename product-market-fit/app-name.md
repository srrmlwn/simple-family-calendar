# App Name Research — famcal.ai

> Goal: Find a name that signals AI, simplifies life for families, and evokes happiness, smiles, and ease.

---

## Naming Criteria

| Criteria | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Signals AI | .ai TLD or AI-adjacent feel; product is AI-native |
| Family-coded | Parents should instantly feel it's for them |
| Happiness / warmth | Smiles, brightness, joy — the app reduces family stress |
| Short & memorable | ≤ 8 chars ideal; easy to say aloud |
| Clean availability | No active competing products; domain registrable |

## Session 3 — Deep Research: simplical.ai (March 2026)

**Proposed name:** "simpli" + "cal" = simplify + calendar → **simplical.ai**

### Verdict: PROCEED WITH CAUTION ⚠️

No competing product named "Simplical" exists anywhere. Domain and social handles appear available. But the "Simpli-" prefix is a crowded trademark neighborhood — get attorney clearance before committing.

| Signal | Finding |
|---|---|
| Competing product | None found — App Store, Play, Product Hunt, Crunchbase all clear |
| simplical.ai domain | No indexed content — likely unregistered |
| simplical.com/io/co/net | All appear unregistered |
| @simplical (X/Instagram/TikTok) | No active accounts found |
| USPTO exact match | No "SIMPLICAL" or "SIMPLI-CAL" trademark found |

**Trademark risk:** SIMPLICAM (SimpliSafe, Class 9) is phonetically very close — `SIM-pli-kam` vs `SIM-pli-kul`, same syllable count, one phoneme apart. A USPTO office action citing SIMPLICAM is likely. SIMPLISAFE owns 22+ active Class 9 registrations and has a litigation track record.

## Session 4 — Deep Research: simplycal.ai (March 2026)

**Proposed name:** "simply" + "cal" → **simplycal.ai**
**Domain:** simplycal.ai confirmed available.

### Verdict: PROCEED WITH CAUTION ⚠️ (same risk as simplical.ai + one extra concern)

| Signal | Finding |
|---|---|
| simplycal.ai | Confirmed available |
| simplycal.com/io/co/net | Appear unregistered; verify via WHOIS |
| simplycal.de | **ACTIVE** — ORGA-SOFT (German calibration software). No US rights found. |
| @simplycal (X/TikTok) | No active accounts found |
| @simplycal (Instagram) | SimplyCal Candles (small artisan brand) active — handle needs direct verification |
| USPTO exact match | No "SIMPLYCAL" or "SIMPLY CAL" trademark found |

**Extra concern:** An actively maintained iOS calendar app called **SimpleCal** (14,000+ ratings, 4.72 stars, Oct 2025) is phonetically nearly identical and in the exact same category. Real consumer confusion risk at launch. This doesn't exist for SIMPLICAL.

**Trademark risk:** Same SIMPLICAM/SIMPLISAFE concerns as SIMPLICAL. The SIMPLY vs. SIMPLI distinction is negligible — USPTO treats them as equivalent.

**Conclusion:** SIMPLICAL has a small edge over SIMPLYCAL specifically because of the SimpleCal iOS app conflict.

## Session 5 — Deep Research: famcierge.ai (March 2026)

**Proposed name:** "fam" + "concierge" → **famcierge.ai**
**Domain:** famcierge.ai confirmed available.

### Verdict: PROCEED WITH CAUTION ⚠️

Strong brand positioning, clean trademark path — but famcierge.com is taken by a real operating business.

| Signal | Finding |
|---|---|
| famcierge.ai | Confirmed available |
| **famcierge.com** | **TAKEN — active NJ elder care company** |
| famcierge.io/co | No indexed content; likely available — verify WHOIS |
| @famcierge (X/Instagram/TikTok) | No active accounts found; appear available |
| USPTO exact match (FAMCIERGE) | **No filings found — clear** |
| FAM + CONCIERGE in Class 9/42 | No registered marks found |
| CONCIERGE family in software | Crowded but weak/descriptive — manageable |

**The headline risk — famcierge.com:**
A real, operating New Jersey elder care business uses famcierge.com. Not a squatter. This means:
- Searching "famcierge" surfaces both your app and a home care company
- Permanently split .com/.ai brand presence
- Potential common-law friction if they ever seek federal registration

**Trademark picture:**
- No USPTO filing exists for FAMCIERGE — the mark is clear to file
- CONCIERGE is crowded in software (REACH CONCIERGE, Moxie CONCIERGE, etc.) but the term is weak/descriptive, giving existing marks narrow protection
- Most likely office action: §2(d) citing REACH CONCIERGE (appointment scheduling app) — winnable with proper attorney response

**Additional conflicts:**
- **TextConcierge.ai** — direct competitor (SMS-based AI family calendar) already uses "concierge" as their brand descriptor. Legal non-issue, but both products pitch "AI concierge for families"
- **famHQ** (famhqconcierge.com) — "family concierge" household app; semantic neighbor, no legal conflict
- **Family Concierge™** (famconcierge.com) — nanny staffing agency using ™ symbol; Class 35/45 (staffing services), not software

**Brand quality:**
FAMCIERGE scored highest of all researched names for positioning strength. "Concierge" implies premium, AI-native, does-the-work-for-you — coherent with the product's core bet. Stronger positioning than smilecal, simplical, or kindai. Mispronunciation of "concierge" is a moderate friction in word-of-mouth contexts.

**Priority action items:**
1. Register @famcierge on X, Instagram, TikTok immediately — before any public signal
2. WHOIS check famcierge.io and famcierge.co
3. Attempt outreach to famcierge.com owner — small regional business may sell for $500–$3,000
4. Engage trademark attorney for Class 9 + 42 filing; budget for potential office action response

Requires: (1) attempt to buy famcierge.com, (2) attorney trademark filing, (3) immediate social handle registration.
